Quality policy

It is the policy of Diamond Subs Explorations Limited to provide a service that satisfies our clients needs, meet the specified qiality requirements that are in accordance with the contarctual specifications, code and statuory requirements. In achieving the goal, the company places importance on the training and elimination of causes and errors with emphasis on preventive maintenance rather reactive. This way we achieve high quality at low costs.
